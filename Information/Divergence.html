2. KL散度 KL divergence

有两个独立的分布P(x)和Q(x)，KL散度用于衡量这两个分布的差异。

DKL(P∣∣Q)=EX∼P[logP(x)Q(x)]=EX∼P[logP(x)−logQ(x)] D_{KL}(P||Q) = E_{X\sim P}\left[\log \frac{P(x)}{Q(x)}\right] = E_{X\sim P}[\log P(x) - \log Q(x)] D​KL​​(P∣∣Q)=E​X∼P​​[log​Q(x)​​P(x)​​]=E​X∼P​​[logP(x)−logQ(x)]

H(P,Q)=−EX∼PlogQ(x) H(P, Q) = -E_{X\sim P} \log Q(x) H(P,Q)=−E​X∼P​​logQ(x)
